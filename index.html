Circle Number Game

Rules for Level 1:

  • You have 25 seconds.
  • Click on the start button to begin.
  • There are 10 circles with a number 1 to 10 hidden underneath.
  • Click the correct circles in order from 1 to 10
  • A circle will turn blue and display its number when you have the right circle.
  • A circle will display red when you click the wrong one and keep its number hidden.
  • You do not have to start over after clicking the wrong one.
  • Each level will get progressively harder.



Good luck!
